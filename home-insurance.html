COVERAGE OPTIONS

What's Covered in Your Home Policy

A standard home insurance policy includes these coverages, with optional add-ons for tailored protection.

Dwelling Coverage

Protects the structure of your home (walls, roof, foundation) from covered perils like fire, wind, and hail.

Personal Property

Covers your belongings (furniture, electronics, clothing) against theft, damage, or loss.

Liability Protection

Covers bodily injury or property damage you're responsible for, including legal fees.

Loss of Use (ALE)

Pays for temporary housing and living expenses if your home becomes uninhabitable.

Other Structures

Covers detached structures like garages, fences, sheds, and gazebos.

Medical Payments

Pays for medical expenses if someone is injured on your property, regardless of liability.
